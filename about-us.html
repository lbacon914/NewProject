How We Met in the 70s

All five of us born and raised in Southern California, met in 10th grade at SouthWest LA Junior High School. We were in band class together one day, when we all realized we had amazing voices as a harmony to start our own first Girls Singing Group. Began, March 17th, 1971 when we made our first hit record "Intro to the Foxxy's." We call ourselves "The Foxxy Five", with fierce accredibility, independent aruras, positive vibes, and all different skin complexions. Performing on day to day basis, taking tours, coming back to visit there hometown, the girls find time to still try and live a normal romantic life. However, the ladies have some many hit songs and fun moments, they rather spend time together with one another then out being apart of society.
